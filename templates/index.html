Euler's Method Calculator

Numerical Method for Approximating Solutions of Ordinary Differential Equations

Description

Euler's Method is a numerical technique for approximating solutions to first-order differential equations. It uses a known starting point and slope to estimate future values step by step.

Formula

\[ y_{n+1}=y_n+h f(x_n,y_n) \]

Worked Example 1

\( dy/dx=x+y \)

\( y(0)=1,\ h=0.1 \)

\( y_1=1.1,\ y_2=1.22,\ y_3=1.362 \)

Worked Example 2

\( dy/dx=x-y \)

\( y(0)=2,\ h=0.2 \)

\( y_1=1.6,\ y_2=1.32,\ y_3=1.136 \)
